## Introduction to Viettel's Development History
The lecture discusses the development history of Viettel, a Vietnamese telecommunications company, from 2010 to 2020. The speaker highlights the key events, milestones, and strategies that defined Viettel's growth during this period.

## Historical Context
The speaker identifies three key historical contexts that shaped Viettel's development:
* **Market saturation**: The traditional telecommunications market was becoming saturated, and Viettel needed to shift its focus to data services to remain competitive.
* **National security**: The need for self-sufficiency in military equipment and national security drove Viettel to develop its own research and production capabilities.
* **International expansion**: Viettel's strategic vision for international expansion and economic diplomacy aimed to promote Vietnam's interests abroad.

## Key Events and Milestones
The speaker highlights several key events and milestones in Viettel's development:
* **2010**: Viettel introduced a new management model, and the company began to focus on research and production of military equipment.
* **2011**: Viettel established a cyber security center and launched a television broadcasting service.
* **2014**: Viettel formalized its cyber security capabilities and established a rapid response team.
* **2015**: Viettel developed a radar system and launched 4G network trials.
* **2017**: Viettel completed the deployment of 36,000 4G base stations, achieving a major milestone in the country's telecommunications history.
* **2018**: Viettel was renamed as a industrial-conglomerate, and its radar system was successfully tested.

## International Expansion
The speaker notes that Viettel's international expansion was a key aspect of its development during this period. The company adopted a strategy of "encircling cities from the countryside" and "popularizing telecommunications services." Today, Viettel operates in 10 countries across three continents, serving millions of customers and leaving a significant mark on the global telecommunications industry.

## Conclusion
The lecture provides a comprehensive overview of Viettel's development history from 2010 to 2020, highlighting the company's key strategies, milestones, and achievements. The speaker emphasizes the importance of understanding the historical context and the company's vision for international expansion in shaping Viettel's growth and success.
